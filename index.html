Institution Profile

A Radiant Path of Quran & Sunnah

Alhamdulillah, Madrasa Arabiyya Taleem Ul Quran is a magnificent religious institution spreading the light of knowledge in society. We equip both male and female students with modern and religious education focusing on moral excellence.

Hifz Education System

Our young Hafiz students excel in modern Arabic conversation, public speaking, and practical moral training.

Future Objectives

  • Construction of modern classrooms and expanding hostel facilities.
  • Dignified and secure educational arrangements for female students.
  • Establishment of modern technology centers alongside religious studies.
  • Implementing a highly organized educational system modeled after Jamia Ashrafia Lahore.
